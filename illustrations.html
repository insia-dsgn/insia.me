CONTEXT

My editorial clients (the Innocence Project, 34th Street Magazine, and The Daily Pennsylvanian) cover a wide range of topics. I create stunning illustrations and photo manipulations to accompany articles, other digital content, and print product to elevate the stories being told, be they centered on victims of an unjust judicial system, student journalism on local Philadelphia news, or pop culture critiques.

My personal illustrations often depict my own emotions and stories, employing vibrant colors, textures, and geometric shapes.
