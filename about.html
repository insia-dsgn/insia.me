Hi, I’m Insia :)

I am an artist and designer from Harlem, New York, studying cognitive science (and a number of other things!) at Penn.

I’m passionate about all things that make us human—the inner workings of the mind, the language we speak, and the things we create to document our lives and pass time. I also love knitting, painting, and crafting in general.

My biggest goal is to create meaningful design for social impact. I previously designed the Innocence Project and 34th Street Magazine, using art and design to draw eyes to the stories that matter.
