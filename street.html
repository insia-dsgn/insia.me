CONTEXT

I oversaw design and illustration at 34th Street Magazine, a Philadelphia-based arts and culture magazine. As design editor, I led the creative direction of our 2025 monthly print issues and digital content. Additionally, I redesigned our Instagram and created graphics for both print and web articles.

Monthly Magazines

Every month, 34th Street Magazine reinvents itself. In 2025, I led that reinvention. As Design Editor, I translated complex editorial themes, from sexual health education to environmental justice, into immersive visual experiences. My work balanced high-level, unique art direction with hands-on graphic design, blending typography, illustration, and photography to create a magazine that is as visually engaging as it is readable. A lot of my package design was inspired by my love for my home city, New York, in tandem with Philadelphia.
